My journey has only been about striving for excellence in the sport and taking India to every tennis game / match possible. I have prepared for success and left nothing to chance. I worked hard, invented myself for the sport and reinvented myself to stay ahead of the curve. My confidence is my preparation and my team who has backed me for the last three decades. It is not the frame, or the build or good fortune that got me all the world records I have earned. The hard work, hours of training and the belief that I can do it has brought me where I am today in my journey. I have defied every norm in tennis.
